About Me

I'm Jacob, a 2D artist and a writer who constantly forgets how to do both. I'm from the Bay Area, currently enrolled in UCSC's AGPM major. Most of my art comes in the form of anime and hopefully semi-realisitc in the future (when i get better at both and stop stumbling over myself). I'm also learning how to animate, and also Blender. My main mediums come in the form of Clip Studio Paint and Paint Tool SAI, though I haven't used the latter in years. My dream is to both fully develop and draw an entire game by myself, without any team to help out.
